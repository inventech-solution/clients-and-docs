## Implementation Notes

1. **Each page targets exactly one primary keyword.** Do not optimise two pages for the same primary keyword — this causes cannibalization.
2. **Primary keyword should appear in:** H1, first paragraph, meta description, and at least one subheading.
3. **Secondary keywords** appear naturally in body copy — do not force them.
4. **Update this map** when new pages are added. Assign a keyword before writing, not after.
5. **Cannibalization check:** if two pages are ranking for the same keyword, consolidate them into one stronger page and redirect the weaker URL.
