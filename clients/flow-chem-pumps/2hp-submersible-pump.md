# 2HP Submersible Pump

Flow Chem Pumps manufactures 2HP submersible pumps for construction dewatering, sewage management, industrial drainage, and wastewater treatment applications. The 2HP power rating sits at the practical centre of our submersible pump range — delivering higher flow rates and head pressure than 1–1.5HP models while remaining portable and suitable for single or three-phase power supply.

## Why 2HP?

The 2HP submersible pump is the most commonly specified pump for mid-scale construction and industrial applications in India. At this power rating:

- Flow rates typically reach 60–100 m³/hr depending on head
- The pump handles moderate to high groundwater inflow in construction sites
- Three-phase models support continuous S1 duty without overheating
- Single-phase variants are available for sites without three-phase power
- The pump size remains portable enough to relocate as site requirements change

For applications requiring more sustained high-volume pumping — large excavations, tunnels, or permanent sewage stations — the 3HP to 15HP range provides increased capacity at proportionally higher power draw.

## Material Selection for Your Environment

**Cast iron** — Standard construction for most dewatering, drainage, and sewage applications with neutral pH water. Cost-effective and proven in general industrial use.

**SS304** — For mildly corrosive wastewater, mildly saline conditions, or food-grade environments requiring stainless contact surfaces.

**SS316** — For highly corrosive wastewater, coastal and saltwater applications, chemical plant effluent, and aggressive pH environments (below 5 or above 9).
