# 3HP Submersible Pump

Flow Chem Pumps manufactures 3HP submersible pumps for heavy-duty construction dewatering, large-scale sewage management, industrial slurry handling, and continuous-duty wastewater treatment applications. The 3HP rating delivers significantly higher flow rates and head capacity than 2HP models, making it the preferred choice for large excavations, permanent pump stations, and industrial environments with sustained high water volumes.

## Where 3HP is the Right Choice

A 3HP submersible pump is typically specified when:

- Peak water inflow exceeds what a 2HP pump can clear comfortably
- The discharge head requirement (vertical lift plus friction losses) is above 15–18 m
- The application demands continuous 24-hour operation under load without motor thermal cutout
- The pumped liquid contains a high concentration of solids, slurry, or abrasive particulates
- The installation is permanent and pump reliability is critical to site or plant operation

For smaller construction sites or light drainage applications, the 1HP or 2HP range provides adequate capacity at lower capital and operating cost. The 3HP pump is not oversized for general site drainage — it is correctly specified for demanding applications.

## Material of Construction

**Cast iron** — Standard for most dewatering, drainage, and neutral-pH sewage applications.

**SS304** — For mildly corrosive wastewater, stainless contact surface requirements, or chemical plant environments with moderate pH variation.

**SS316** — For aggressive chemical environments, saline or coastal applications, and high-corrosivity wastewater with pH below 5 or above 9.
