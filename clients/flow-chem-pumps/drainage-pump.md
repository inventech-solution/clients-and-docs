# Drainage Pump

Flow Chem Pumps manufactures submersible drainage pumps designed for efficient removal of water from residential, commercial, and industrial environments. Built for reliability and continuous operation, our drainage pumps handle clean water, lightly contaminated water, and water carrying fine suspended solids.

## What is a Drainage Pump?

A drainage pump removes excess or unwanted water from a specific location — basements, waterlogged land, construction excavations, retention pits, and flooded areas. Unlike sewage pumps built for solid-laden waste, drainage pumps are optimised for high flow rates with water that is relatively clean or contains only fine particulates.

Submersible drainage pumps operate fully submerged, which means no priming, quieter operation, and the ability to work in confined or deep spaces where surface-mounted pumps cannot reach.

## Applications

**Construction and civil works** — Removes surface water and groundwater seepage from excavation sites, keeping work areas safe and dry during foundation and structural work.

**Basement and underground drainage** — Clears accumulated water from basements, parking structures, underpasses, and underground utilities after flooding or heavy rainfall.

**Agricultural drainage** — Manages waterlogging in fields and farm channels, protecting crops from root damage caused by standing water.

**Municipal stormwater** — Handles stormwater overflow in drainage channels, retention ponds, and low-lying urban zones during peak rainfall events.

**Industrial floor drainage** — Removes wash-down water, cooling water, and incidental process water from factory floors, loading docks, and storage areas.

**Emergency flood response** — Fast-deployment pumping for flood-affected residential and commercial properties.

## Single Phase and Three Phase Options

Flow Chem drainage pumps are available in both single phase and three phase configurations:

- **Single phase (1 HP – 2 HP)** — suitable for light commercial and residential applications where three-phase power is unavailable
- **Three phase (2 HP – 7.5 HP)** — recommended for industrial and construction applications requiring sustained high-flow operation

Three-phase models offer better motor efficiency, lower heat generation, and longer service life under continuous duty conditions.
