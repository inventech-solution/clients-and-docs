## Understanding Submersible Pumps

### The Basics of Operation

Submersible pumps, as their name implies, operate underwater. They are designed to push fluid to the surface, utilizing direct pressure mechanisms rather than relying on suction. This section of the blog will detail how these pumps manage to remain efficient and operational even under harsh conditions.

### Design and Components

Diving deeper, we will explore the integral parts of a submersible pump, including the motor, impellers, and the seals that protect its electrical components.
