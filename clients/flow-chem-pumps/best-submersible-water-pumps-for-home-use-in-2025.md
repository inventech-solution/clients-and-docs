### What is a Submersible Pump?

A submersible pump is a pumping device designed to operate while submerged in water. It uses a sealed motor to prevent water ingress, ensuring efficient and safe operation.

### How Does a Submersible Pump Work?

* The pump is placed directly in the water source (e.g., a well or sump).

* Water enters the pump through an intake valve and is pushed upward by the impeller.

* It uses direct water contact for cooling, eliminating the need for external cooling systems.
