## Understanding Submersible Pumps

Submersible pumps are devices designed to operate underwater, making them ideal for various disaster relief applications. They consist of several key components:

* Motor: Encased in a waterproof seal to function underwater.

* Impeller: Moves the water through the pump.

* Casing: Protects internal components and directs water flow.

* Seals and Bearings: Ensure the pump operates efficiently without water ingress.

## How a Submersible Pump Works

The submersible pump operates by converting rotational energy into kinetic energy, which then forces water to the surface. The pump's motor, sealed and waterproof, drives an impeller, creating a centrifugal force that propels water through the pump and out through the discharge pipe.
