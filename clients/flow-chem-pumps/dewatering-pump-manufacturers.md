## What We Manufacture

### Submersible dewatering pumps
Heavy-duty pumps for construction site dewatering, mining water management, tunnel drainage, and flood control. Available from 1 HP to 15 HP with capacity up to 200 m³/hr and head up to 25 m.

### Submersible sewage / sludge pumps
Designed for continuous duty in ETP, STP, and CETP applications. Handles wastewater with solids up to 70 mm, thick sludge, and high-solid-content sewage in treatment plant circuits.

### Submersible drainage pumps
Optimised for high flow rate water removal in construction, stormwater management, industrial drainage, and emergency flood response.

### Submersible cutter pumps
Fitted with hardened steel cutting mechanisms for sewage containing fibrous materials, rags, and mixed solid waste. Used in municipal lift stations, commercial buildings, and industrial effluent systems.

### Submersible agitator slurry pumps
Equipped with a base agitator to keep settled slurry in suspension before pumping. Used in mining, dredging, and industrial sludge management.

### Submersible jet aerator pumps
For aeration in biological wastewater treatment — activated sludge and extended aeration plant designs.
