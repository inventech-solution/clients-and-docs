### Introduction (Target: 130–160 words)

Government bodies, municipal corporations, and public sector undertakings manage some of the largest document repositories in the country — land records, birth and death certificates, municipal permits, procurement files, HR records, and decades of institutional correspondence. Most of this is still in physical form.

India's push toward e-governance, Digital India, and paperless administration has created both the mandate and the urgency to digitise these archives — accurately, securely, and at scale.

Docuforte is a trusted document digitisation and management platform for government organisations. We have the capacity, technology, and operational expertise to handle large-scale digitisation projects — scanning physical records, converting them to searchable digital formats, and deploying a secure, AI-powered document management system that enables authorised staff to retrieve any record instantly.

From municipal corporations to central government departments, Docuforte supports India's transition to digital public administration.

### H2: How Docuforte Works for Government Digitisation Projects

1. **Project Scoping** — We assess the volume, condition, and format of your physical records to design a digitisation project plan
2. **Bulk Scanning and Digitisation** — Our team digitises records at your premises or at our secure facility, with strict chain-of-custody controls
3. **Metadata Tagging and Indexing** — Each document is tagged with the metadata your department requires — file number, date, location, document type
4. **Quality Control** — Every batch is quality-checked for scan accuracy, completeness, and metadata integrity before delivery
5. **DMS Deployment** — Records are uploaded to your Docuforte workspace — on-premises, private cloud, or AWS — with access controls configured by department
6. **Handover and Training** — Your staff receive full training on the platform before go-live
