### H2: Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can Docuforte handle records going back several decades?**
A: Yes. Our bulk scanning and digitisation service is designed for large-volume historical archives. Records are scanned, quality-checked, and tagged with metadata for reliable retrieval regardless of age.

**Q: How does Docuforte protect student data privacy?**
A: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Role-based access controls restrict document visibility to authorised staff only. Docuforte supports compliance with applicable data privacy obligations.

**Q: Can students access their own records through Docuforte?**
A: Yes. A secure student-facing portal can be configured to allow students to request and receive their own documents — with institution approval workflows built in.

**Q: Is Docuforte suitable for both schools and universities?**
A: Absolutely. Docuforte scales from small schools and coaching institutes to large universities and multi-campus educational groups. The platform adapts to your document volume and organisational structure.
