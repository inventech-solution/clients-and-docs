### Introduction (Target: 120–150 words)

Managing patient records, prescriptions, lab reports, and compliance documents is one of the biggest operational challenges in healthcare. Paper-based systems slow down workflows, create compliance risk, and make it difficult to retrieve records when it matters most.

Docuforte is a purpose-built document management system for healthcare organisations — from multi-speciality hospitals and diagnostic labs to clinics and insurance providers. Our platform digitises physical records, organises them in a searchable cloud archive, and gives your teams instant access to the right document at the right time — with full audit trails and role-based access controls to ensure data security and regulatory compliance.

**H3: Instant Patient Record Retrieval**
