### Introduction (Target: 120–150 words)

Financial institutions generate and manage enormous volumes of documents — KYC records, loan files, account opening forms, audit reports, regulatory filings, and transaction correspondence. Managing these manually creates compliance risk, operational inefficiency, and significant overhead.

Docuforte gives banks, NBFCs, insurance companies, and financial services firms a secure, AI-powered platform to digitise, organise, and retrieve financial documents instantly — with the compliance controls, audit trails, and access management that regulators demand.

From bulk digitisation of legacy loan archives to real-time document ingestion for new customer onboarding, Docuforte covers the entire document lifecycle for financial services — so your teams spend less time managing paper and more time serving customers.
