# Docuforte Medium Strategy

Builds topical authority and parasite SEO for Docuforte's document digitisation and DMS platform. Medium's high domain authority accelerates rankings for target keywords; content attracts backlinks and drives qualified traffic to Docuforte.com.

**When invoking**: On **first use**, open with 1–2 sentences on what this covers. On **subsequent use**, go directly to output.

**Client:** Docuforte — B2B + B2G document management SaaS
**Goal:** Drive views & leads via Medium → build backlinks → rank for core keywords
**Target Segments:** Government bodies, Healthcare, Legal, Finance, SMEs, Enterprises
**Core Keywords:** Government document digitization India, Municipal record digitization, Bulk scanning services, DMS software, Land record scanning, Paperless office workflows

## Content Best Practices

- **Keywords**: Target long-tail intent keywords from confirmed list (government digitization, municipal records, bulk scanning, land record scanning, etc.)
- **Length**: 1,500–2,500 words (Medium favors depth; better for SEO + readability)
- **Structure**: Clear H2/H3 headings, short paragraphs, 1–2 visuals, numbered/bulleted lists
- **Tone**: Professional but accessible; avoid jargon unless explained; speak to decision-makers (CXOs, IT heads, procurement)
- **CTA**: Subtle internal links to relevant Docuforte pages (DMS features, government solutions, compliance docs); final CTA to case study or product demo page
- **Canonical**: Set to docuforte.com equivalent (if republishing) to signal original source

## Internal Linking Strategy

**Link from Medium to these Docuforte pages:**
- Government Solutions / B2G landing page
- DMS Features & Pricing
- Compliance & Security page
- Healthcare / Legal / Finance solution pages
- Case Studies (if available)
- Demo / Product page

**Don't over-link** — 3–5 contextual internal links per article; prioritize first mention and natural placement.

## CTA Placement

- **Early (intro)**: Soft mention of Docuforte's relevance ("platform like Docuforte helps...")
- **Mid (case study / pain point)**: Linked example or feature callout
- **End (call-to-action)**: "See how Docuforte solves this" → demo/case study link
