## CAMPAIGN PERFORMANCE METRICS

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA):** Tracked by product category and audience segment
- **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS):** Target 3:1–5:1 depending on season and category
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** 1.5–3% benchmark for apparel
- **Conversion Rate (CVR):** 2–4% from ad to purchase
- **Average Order Value (AOV):** ₹2,500–₹4,500 (multi-item baskets)
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV):** Repeat purchase tracking, seasonal loyalty

### Budget Allocation Strategy
- **60% – Bestsellers:** Co-ords, Oversized T-Shirts, Hero products
- **25% – Seasonal/New Launches:** Summer collection, limited drops
- **15% – Niche/Experiment:** Testing new audiences, bundle combos, retention campaigns
