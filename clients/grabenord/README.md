## 2. What They Sell

Grabenord is a plant-based food brand offering dairy-free, vegan, cholesterol-free, and lactose-free alternatives across the following product lines:

- **Butter** — Salted, Unsalted, Garlic
- **Cheese** — Parmesan, Cheddar, and other variants
- **Cream Cheese**
- **Basil Pesto**
- **Kombucha**

**Positioning:** Premium plant-based alternatives for health-conscious, vegan, and lactose-intolerant consumers who don't want to compromise on taste.

---

## 3. Target Audience

Consumers who actively seek out:
- Plant-based / vegan food products
- Cholesterol-free and dairy-free alternatives
- Lactose-free options

**Psychographic profile:** Health-aware, label-readers, likely urban, willing to pay a premium for clean-label products. Discovered through both intent-driven search (Google, marketplace) and awareness-driven channels (Meta, social communities).

---

## 4. Core Business Challenge

**Go omni-channel with profitability.**

Grabenord is at a scale inflection point — they have traction on quick-commerce (Swiggy Instamart, BigBasket) but need to expand across D2C (Shopify), paid social, search, and organic channels without sacrificing margin. The challenge is building a full-funnel engine where every channel pulls its weight and compounds rather than cannibalising.
