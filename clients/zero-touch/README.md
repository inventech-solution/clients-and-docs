## 3. Target Audience

**Buyer Type:** B2C

**Primary Segments:**
- Personal hygiene enthusiasts
- Home renovators and interior designers
- Smart home adopters
- Urban, upwardly mobile consumers (Pan-India)

**Geography:** Pan-India

**Buying behaviour:** Discovery-led purchase — customers typically don't search for bidets; they discover them through social ads, influencer content, or marketplace browsing. Impulse-to-consideration conversion cycle. Strong post-purchase retention opportunity given product category (hygiene routine = repeat engagement).

## 4. Core Business Challenge

**Scale revenue from ₹10–15L to ₹75L/month.**

A 5–7x revenue growth target. This requires:
- Performance marketing at higher scale without ROAS degradation
- A retention engine that converts first-time buyers into repeat customers
- CRO improvements to increase conversion rate and AOV on Shopify
- Retention metrics and CRO metrics are not yet being tracked — this is a gap that needs to be closed immediately

### Current Benchmarks

| Metric | Current | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Revenue | ₹10–15L | ₹75L |
| ROAS | 4x | Maintain or improve at scale |
| CPA | ₹650 | ₹600 |
| AOV | ₹3,215.86 | Increase via bundles / upsells |
| Returning Customer Rate | 10.86% | Improve via retention flows |
| CVR (Shopify) | 📌 Not tracked yet | 📌 Establish baseline |

> 💡 **AOV vs Selling Price Insight:** AOV (₹3,215.86) is significantly higher than the hero product price (₹2,700) — customers are already buying more than one item per order. This is a strong signal for bundle and upsell opportunities in CRO.

> 💡 **Retention Opportunity:** A 10.86% returning customer rate is a low baseline for a hygiene product with daily use. There's meaningful upside from WhatsApp retention flows targeting repeat purchases and cross-sells (bidet → portable bidet → bed vacuum).

## 11. Constraints & Notes

- Product category (bidet, hygiene) requires careful creative direction — ads and copy need to be tasteful, benefit-focused, and not awkward or clinical
- Discovery-led category — most buyers don't know they want a bidet until they see it; top-of-funnel awareness creative is critical, not just conversion ads
- Scaling 5–7x is aggressive — ROAS will likely compress as spend increases; set realistic expectations with Venkat around acceptable ROAS floor at scale
- Influencer / UGC pipeline needs to stay active to feed creative refresh and avoid ad fatigue at higher spends
- WhatsApp (Interakt) is the only confirmed retention channel — email should be evaluated as an additional channel
